About Us

About our mission

Science has been leading the world to progress throughout the history of humanity. Great scientists led science with paradigm-shifting inventions. The world needs young and energetic minds to take us forward with significant contributions in different walks of science. "GALAPAGOS" The Life Science Island is a unique ecosystem for science enthusiasts to metamorphos.

But getting a fellowship and clearing the PhD interview is the main hurdle for any young researcher. With our mentors and faculties trained in national premier institutes and international institutes we provide the students an unmatched environment to evolve as a budding researcher. We equip our students to tackle this challenge by training them to qualify for national, international fellowships and interviews at any level. Let's join to grow together in a facilitative ecosystem.

"Quality Education for All"

We believe education is a human right, and money should not be a barrier for anybody.
