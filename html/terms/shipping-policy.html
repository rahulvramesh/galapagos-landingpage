Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy

Galapagos Education is dedicated to delivering your order promptly while maintaining top-quality packaging. All orders are shipped within a week and typically arrive within 4-5 working days from dispatch. However, deliveries to remote locations or later delays due to other circumstances beyond our control, such as strikes, natural disasters, government actions etc. We ship every day of the week, except on public holidays, during government-enforced lockdowns, or other situations preventing delivery operations. To ensure timely and secure delivery, we partner with India Post.

Delivery Charges

Delivery charges vary based on cart value and location.

Delivery Time

Orders usually arrive within two to three weeks

Communication with Students

Customers will receive updates about their orders via WhatsApp broadcasts from Galapagos Education. Students can also contact us at our support number for assistance.

Damaged Product Policy

If you receive a damaged product, contact your mentor or call our helpline. We will verify the details and proceed with the replacement process.
