Courses

Crack-NET and Bag-JRF

"Learning how to learn is the real learning process". Our comprehensive coaching program, designed to prepare you for various exams, including CSIR Life Science, GATE Life Science, GATE Ecology and Evolution, GATE Biotechnology, DBT JRF, and ICMR JRF, offers a unique learning experience. With our concept-centric learning module, you can gain a deep understanding of the subjects and be confident in your readiness for these challenging exams. We design our classes scientifically to follow the flow of evolution so that one can quickly grasp the concepts; hence, there is no need to mug up things anymore.

International Research Program Entry

If you are keenly looking for a PhD or a sponsored research-oriented master's program abroad, here is your piece of bread. Our International Research Program Entry (IPRE) training will equip you to prepare your application with a robust research statement, Statement of Purpose, Motivation letter and a compelling CV. A strong PhD/Masters application should have all three above and more... What more???

National Premier Institute (NPI) PhD

Are you eyeing IISc, IISER, IIT, NISER, NCBS, TIFR, CCMB, NIT, etc., for a PhD?? Can a mere JRF help you get through their interviews?? Prepping for the National Premier Institute PhD interview is no longer a Himalayan task. Galapagos helps you prepare for the interview with a series of mock interviews and will tackle the areas you need to focus on. Our mock interview panel will have researchers who had previously qualified PhD interviews at different National Premier Institutes. Meet our esteemed panel of mentors.

International Fellowship Application (IFA)

Have you ever thought of getting a prestigious international fellowship for your studies?? Who doesn't want their CV to be of a DAAD fellow, ERASMUS Scholar, or NEWTON awardee? However, International Fellowship Applications are only possible for students to fulfil with proper orientation. GALAPAGOS makes you confident in preparing a super-strong application with appropriate orientation and interactive sessions with former awardees of the prestigious fellowships. We have a pool of mentors with countless international fellowships and will make you the next in the queue.
