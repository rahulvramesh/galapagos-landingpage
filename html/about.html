About our mission

Science is the greatest force propelling the progress of mankind. The curiosity to conquer the unknown and the desire to change the world have inspired great minds throughout the history of humanity, leading to paradigm-shifting discoveries and inventions. In this era of knowledge and information, there is an ever-expanding need for young and energetic minds to join the grand endeavour of scientific research and take the baton forward. "GALAPAGOS" The Life Science Island is a unique ecosystem for young science enthusiasts to learn, aspire, and metamorphose.

Securing a fellowship and excelling in the interviews are main hurdles in front of any young researcher aspiring to enrol in a PhD program. With mentors and faculties trained at premier institutes in India and abroad, we provide the students with an unmatched environment to evolve as a budding researcher. We equip our students to be competent for national and international research fellowships, prepare them to excel in interviews at any level, and provide guidance right through their journey into their favourite area of research.

Let's join to grow together in a facilitative ecosystem.
