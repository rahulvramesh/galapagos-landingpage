Renjith MR

Renjith MR

Post-doc Queen Mary university London

Dr Renjith M R is currently a postdoctoral researcher at Queen Mary University of London. He completed his PhD at IISER Trivandrum, spanning from 2015 to 2022. Renjith's research interests are diverse and encompass mitosis, the cytoskeleton, kinetochore-microtubule interactions, meiosis mechanisms, centromere biology, and genetics and molecular interactions. His academic journey is marked by significant achievements, including qualifying for UGC-JRF in 2013 and 2014, GATE Life Sciences in 2014, CSIR NET in 2013, DBT JRF in 2013, and GATE Biotechnology in 2012. These qualifications underscore his expertise and dedication to advancing the field of cellular and molecular biology.
